Pepper Jack Bacon Ranch Dip
287
1 hour 15 minutes
ohsweetbasil.com
>5 slices thick cut bacon diced into 1 in. pieces
>1 16 ounce container sour cream
>1 3/4 ounce package ranch dressing mix
>1 1/2 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
>3 green onions diced
>1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
done
>Cook bacon in a skillet over medium heat until browned and crispy.
>Mix together sour cream, ranch dip, pepper jack cheese, green onions and dill.
>Fold in bacon and mix well.
>Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
>Serve with chips or chopped vegetables
