Ham and Swiss Omelet
530
20 minutes
tasteofhome.com
>1 tablespoon butter
>3 eggs
>3 tablespoons water
>1/8 teaspoon salt
>1/8 teaspoon pepper
>1/2 cup cubed fully cooked ham
>1/4 cup shredded Swiss cheese
done
>In a small nonstick skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. Whisk the eggs, water, salt and pepper. Add egg mixture to skillet (mixture should set immediately at edges).
>As eggs set, push cooked edges toward the center, letting uncooked portion flow underneath.
>When the eggs are set, place ham on one side and sprinkle with cheese; fold other side over filling. Slide omelet onto a plate.
