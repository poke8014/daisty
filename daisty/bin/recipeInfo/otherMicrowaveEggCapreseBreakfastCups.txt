Microwave Egg Caprese Breakfast Cups
300
10 minutes
foodiecrush.com
>2 slices thinly sliced ham
>Shredded mozzarella or provolone or a mix of the two
>2 eggs
>Basil pesto sauce
>Cherry tomatoes cut in half
>Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
>Fresh basil leaves if desired
done
>Layer the slices of ham on the bottom and up the sides of a ramekin or small bowl pressing into the creases and ruffling the edges then sprinkle with the cheese.
>Crack the eggs into the ramekins and add a dollop of pesto over the eggs with a few cherry tomato halves and season with kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper.
>Place in the microwave and cover with a microwave plate cover and cook on high for 1 minute and 30 seconds or until the whites are set, adding additional 20 second bursts if needed.
>Garnish with fresh basil leaves and more kosher salt and black pepper if desired.
