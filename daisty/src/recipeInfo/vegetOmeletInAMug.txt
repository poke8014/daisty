Omelet in a Mug
160
5 minutes
dashingdish.com
>1 Egg
>2 Egg white
>2 tbs Almond milk
>Pinch of Salt and pepper
>2 tbs Roma tomatoes
>2 tbs Shredded Cheese
done
>Spray a microwave safe mug with cooking spray. Beat together eggs, milk, seasoning, tomatoes and cheese in the mug.
>Microwave, uncovered, on high for 1 minute. Remove mug and stir. Cook for an additional 1 minute, or until eggs are completely set! Remove from microwave and top with a sprinkle of additional cheese if desired.
>Let sit for a few minutes before enjoying, or take with you on the go!
