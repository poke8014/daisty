Stuffed Asiago-Basil Mushrooms
35
35 minutes
tasteofhome.com
>24 baby portobello mushrooms (about 1 pound), stems removed
>1/2 cup reduced-fat mayonnaise
>3/4 cup shredded Asiago cheese
>1/2 cup loosely packed basil leaves, stems removed
>1/4 teaspoon white pepper
>12 cherry tomatoes, halved
>Thinly sliced or shaved Parmesan cheese, optional
done
Preheat oven to 375Â°.
Place mushroom caps in a greased 15x10x1-in. baking pan.
Bake 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, place mayonnaise, Asiago cheese, basil and pepper in a food processor; process until blended.
Drain juices from mushrooms.
Fill each with 1 rounded teaspoon mayonnaise mixture; top each with a tomato half.
Bake until lightly browned, 8-10 minutes.
If desired, top with Parmesan cheese.
