No-cook chicken Banh mi
440
15 minutes
taste.com.au
>2 Coles Bakery Vietnamese Rolls or hot dog rolls
>1/4 cup whole-egg mayonnaise
>1/4 Coles Hot Roast Chicken, coarsely shredded
>100g pkt Coles Beetroot Slaw
>0.5 tbsp sriracha or chilli sauce (optional)
>4 long coriander sprigs, trimmed
done
>Split each bread roll in half without cutting all the way through. Spread cut sides with mayonnaise.
>Fill with chicken and beetroot slaw. Drizzle with chilli sauce, if desired. Serve with coriander sprigs.
